Solvent Miscibility Chart

Quick reference guide for solvent compatibility in extractions and chromatography
1. Overview
What is this tool?

The Solvent Miscibility Chart provides an interactive grid displaying the mixing behavior of common laboratory solvents. This data is essential for designing liquid-liquid extractions (workups) and selecting mobile phases for chromatography.

2. Reading the Chart
Symbols & Color Codes

  • X Red Cross (Immiscible): Indicates that the solvents do not mix and will separate into two distinct phases (ideal for partitioning).
3. Interactive Features
Navigation Tools
  • Crosshair Highlighting: As you move your mouse over the grid, the tool automatically highlights the entire row and column for the solvent pair under your cursor. This makes it easy to trace connections across the large grid without losing your place.
  • Detailed Tooltips: Hover your mouse over any specific cell to see a popup message explicitly stating the pair and their status (e.g., "Water + Dichloromethane: Immiscible").
